How do I access the Surveys page?

To access the Surveys page and begin your engagement with our healthcare improvement initiatives, follow these simple steps:

  1. Login to Your Dashboard:
    • Visit the dashboard login page and enter your credentials (username and password).
  2. Main Menu Navigation:
    • Once logged in, you'll find the main menu on the dashboard. Look for the "Surveys" option among the main navigation links.
  3. Click on "Surveys":
    • Select the "Surveys" option from the main menu. This action will direct you to the dedicated Surveys page.
  4. Explore Available Surveys:
    • On the Surveys page, you'll find a collection of small cards representing various surveys. Click on a card to explore details and start participating.
  5. Utilize Filters for Customization:
    • To streamline your survey experience, use the filtering options provided. Filter surveys by status (To Do or Completed) and explore specific categories (Overview, Short surveys, Long surveys, Mixed, and Report) using the respective tabs.
  6. Monitor Overall Progress:
    • Keep an eye on your overall survey progress with the donut graph displayed on the Surveys page. This visual representation provides a quick overview of your contributions.

Remember, your participation is crucial in shaping the future of healthcare. If you have any further questions or encounter issues, feel free to explore our Support section or reach out to our support team for assistance.
